How to read the mm column. 17″ 1080p is your delivery laptop (~127 ppi). A3 is the print target Andre asked for. A4 numbers are listed only to confirm A4 is too small — readable-text minimum in print is around 1.5–2 mm. Anything below that is unreadable. So --fs-micro: 12px is the floor of the system: at 1.6 mm on A3 it just clears the readability line, and on A4 it would not.

§ 06

Card preview · the proposed atom

.card / .card.is-accent / .card.is-rejected
surrogate · GP

Kriging — anchor surrogate

Native posterior mean and variance; direct comparability with K-RVEA and MOEA/D-EGO. Cubic in N — limited for larger online datasets.

role: anchor · σ²: native · scaling: O(N³)

surrogate · transition target

SSGP — primary online surrogate

Approximate GP uncertainty with improved scaling. Random Fourier features, per-target ARD, PRESS τ-calibration via leave-one-out residuals.

role: online · σ²: approx · scaling: O(M³)

surrogate · legacy

ELM family

Engineering baseline. Fast fit and predict, supports incremental training (OS-ELM, EOS-ELM, Ensemble-ELM). No native uncertainty.

role: deterministic · σ²: none · status: legacy

≥ 0.33 Cliff's δ · medium effect

Falsification threshold for sub-question 1: across ≥3 cases and ≥30 seeds, run-time switching must beat fixed NSGA-II / RVEA / random-switch.

3 / 3 contract knobs · τ  λ  θ

Minimal complete knob set. One per channel: surrogate→MOEA (τ), MOEA→acquisition (λ), acquisition/MOEA→surrogate (θ).

status · current focus

≥ 0.6 Cohen's κ · annotator agreement

Trajectory motif κ < 0.6 → motifs reported as exploratory rather than confirmatory cross-case CAS evidence.
