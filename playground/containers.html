page 02 — why SAEA .panel.is-axis · .panel.is-premise
m-axis · failure

m ≥ 4

Exact EHVI becomes prohibitive; BO falls back to Monte-Carlo with high variance. Decomposition or reference-vector MOEAs scale better.

Daulton 2020 · Emmerich 2005

q-axis · failure

q ≈ 10

Batch BO acquisition becomes the bottleneck. Evolutionary infill of q=100 candidates is cheap by comparison.

BoTorch parallel-q wall · Yu 2023

d-axis · failure

d ≈ 30

TPE / GP marginal-likelihood degrades; mixed-integer variables push BO further out of its comfort zone.

Cruz 2024 review

premise · left

Expensive simulation

EnergyPlus annual: minutes-hours. Radiance daylight: minutes. CFD wind: hours. Multi-objective stacks the cost.

premise · right

High-d mixed search

Continuous sliders, categorical envelopes, integer counts. Evolutionary operators handle this natively.

page 03 — surrogate spectrum .panel.is-reg + .panel.is-rejected
surrogate · GP anchor

Kriging

Native posterior mean and variance. Direct comparability with K-RVEA and MOEA/D-EGO. Cubic in N.

σ² native · O(N³) · anchor

surrogate · transition

SSGP

Approximate GP uncertainty with improved scaling. Random Fourier features · per-target ARD · PRESS τ-calibration via LOO.

σ² approx · O(M³) · primary online

surrogate · legacy

ELM family

OS-ELM / EOS-ELM / Ensemble-ELM / classic ELM. Fast incremental fit; no native uncertainty. Engineering baseline only.

σ² post-hoc · status: legacy

page 04 — acquisition strategies .panel.is-acq
bayesian

EI · UCB · ParEGO

Surrogate uncertainty enters the score directly. Algorithm-agnostic — works under any MOEA host.

moo-coupled

Variance + rank

Uses non-dominated rank, crowding distance, population state. Signals BO simply doesn't have access to.

default

Mixed (λ-controlled)

Within batch q, exploit/explore operators are weighted by λ from the contract. Falls back to maximin distance when σ² unreliable.

λ ∈ [0,1] · contract knob 2

page 05 — MOO algorithms .panel.is-alg
m = 2-3

NSGA-II

Non-dominated sort + crowding. Canonical baseline. Used as fixed-solver comparator in falsification trials.

m = 3-10

NSGA-III · RVEA

Reference-point and reference-vector selection. Many-objective extension beyond crowding.

m = 2-4

MOEA/D-DE

Decomposition into scalar sub-problems. Alternative to dominance-based selection.

page 06 — coordination contract .panel.is-knob · .panel.is-lineage
knob 1 · τ

Probabilistic dominance

P(A ≻ B | μ, σ) ≥ τ. τ=1 → deterministic; τ=0.5 → expected dominance suffices.

surrogate → MOEA channel

knob 2 · λ

Exploit-explore balance

λ = f(niche, stagnation). MOEA state controls acquisition mixture.

MOEA → acquisition channel

knob 3 · θ

Perturbation retrain

Refresh when ∑(μ̂−f_true)²/N_recent > θ. Auditable discrete event.

acquisition/MOEA → surrogate

§ 03

Band · horizontal banners

.band.is-claim · .band.is-banner
claim

Surrogates solve evaluation scarcity; MOO operators solve search scarcity — BO bundles both jobs into one engine, and that engine breaks at the q, m, and mixed-variable thresholds.

thesis
Optimisation should be designed as an adaptive system, not a fixed algorithmic pipeline — surrogate, acquisition and MOEA modules communicating through an explicit contract.
