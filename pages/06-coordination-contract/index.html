COORDINATION CONTRACT CHAPTER 4  ·  THESIS

thesis  ·  the unnamed protocol behind host-coupled SAEAs

Three modules, one conversation.

Existing host-coupled SAEAs (K-RVEA, MOEA/D-EGO, probabilistic-dominance NSGA-II) hard-code specific signals between surrogate, acquisition and MOEA — but the literature has not named the underlying protocol. This work lifts the implicit handshake into an explicit communication contract, with CAS as the framing.

claim  ·  the real research question
Exposing observables and knobs is the engineering half. The research half is how each module self-adjusts when it reads the others' state — how a surrogate retrains on niche stagnation, how acquisition turns niche history into a weighted λ distribution, how an MOEA balances σ²-aware probabilistic dominance against confirmed solutions — interface is contract, algorithm is adaptation, the Pareto Front itself is emergent.
