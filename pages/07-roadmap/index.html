A 36-month roadmap.

One year per CAS branch — surrogate (Year 1, in progress), algorithm adaptation (Year 2), emergence & dynamics (Year 3). Each year produces one or two peer-reviewed papers; the framework consolidates into a thesis-by-monograph at submission. Publication strategy: four journal targets — one algorithm-side, two architectural-engineering, one tools — plus annual conference milestones.
