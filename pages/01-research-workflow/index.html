Regime
Pure

Skips Phases 02–03 entirely. The EA evaluates every individual through the true simulation; no surrogate exists, so post-hoc verification is unnecessary.

 Offline

Builds the surrogate once from LHS samples. Optimisation runs purely against the predictions; no infill, no retraining during the search.

 Online

Burgundy infill loop active: every K generations, top candidates are evaluated by the true simulation and fed back to retrain the surrogate (M₀ → M₁ → M₂ …).
